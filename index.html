Who I Am

A Life Dedicated
to Service

Aaliyar Khizer Ahmed is a prominent political leader, social activist, and entrepreneur based in Pernambut, Tamil Nadu. With a deep-rooted commitment to grassroots development and people-centric governance, he has spent over a decade serving his community with unwavering dedication.

As an active member of the Dravida Munnetra Kazhagam (DMK), he works tirelessly at the grassroots level — connecting with constituents, advocating for local needs, and participating in the democratic processes that shape Tamil Nadu's future.

Beyond politics, Khizer Ahmed serves as the President of the Rotary Club of Pernambut, spearheading social service initiatives ranging from health camps and educational support to environmental awareness. He holds a Bachelor of Arts (B.A.) degree and combines academic foundation with practical leadership.

His entrepreneurial spirit is evident in his successful business ventures — the Mango Mandi and AAB Vegetables — which contribute to the agricultural and retail economy of the region, providing livelihoods to local communities.

Leading Community Service
with Purpose

As the President of the Rotary Club of Pernambut, Aaliyar Khizer Ahmed leads a dedicated team of volunteers and professionals committed to humanitarian service. He brings vision, energy, and a deep sense of responsibility to every initiative.

Under his presidency, the Rotary Club has expanded its reach — organizing blood donation camps, educational programs, environmental drives, and community upliftment activities that directly benefit thousands of residents in and around Pernambut.

Guided by Rotary's motto of "Service Above Self", Khizer Ahmed exemplifies what it means to put community needs before personal gain — a quality that defines his entire public life.
